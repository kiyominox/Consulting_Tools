How to use this tool (daily workflow)
  1. Select the store (auto-detected from the schedule paste when possible) and enter the cash date.
  2. In CDK, run Accounting Schedule 1 — CASH CLEARING (no zero-balance controls, include post-ahead), open the export, copy columns A:F, and paste into the CDK Schedule box. Or drop a .csv/.tsv/.txt file.
  3. From the payment processor site, export the day's transactions, select all, copy, and paste into the CC Report box.
  4. Enter the actual deposit totals by type.
  5. Click Run Unfound Match — CC transactions not on the schedule appear under Not On Schedule. Fix any wrong reference numbers there (the amount/note re-match as you type).
  6. For schedule items paid outside the processor, type Y (or the actual amount) in the Received column. CC-matched rows are received automatically.
  7. Record items missing from the schedule entirely under BALFWD Entries (closed RO/invoice, amount, close date).
  8. When the Variance is 0.00, click Generate Posting, then copy the AGJE lines and paste into CDK AGJE (Expert Mode, right-click → Paste).
  9. Click Export Record to download the reconciliation record for record keeping. Save Session lets you resume later.
