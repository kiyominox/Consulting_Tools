How to use this tool — daily workflow
  1. Pick the store (auto-detected from the schedule when possible). The cash date fills in automatically from the processor reports — adjust it only if it's wrong. Accounts and Not-On-Schedule categories update to match the store.
  2. Upload the CDK Cash Clearing schedule export, then drop the processor's card, cash and check reports together — they add up, and the file list shows each one with a remove button (Excel, CSV or text).
  3. Enter the actual deposit totals by type.
  4. In the schedule grid, CC-matched items are received automatically (green/amber). For anything received outside the processor, click “Received in full” or type the exact amount. When a payment came in above the schedule amount as a donation — rounded up to the next dollar or a custom amount the customer typed — the row shows a 🎁 suggestion with the difference; tick it and that amount posts to the donation account/control from ⚙ Setup (default: back to the clearing account under control DONATION).
  5. Click Run Unfound Match; for each Not-On-Schedule item pick the category (where applicable) and correct any wrong reference number.
  6. When Variance is 0.00, click Generate Posting, then Copy AGJE Lines into CDK AGJE (Expert Mode, right-click → Paste).
  7. Export a PDF or Excel record for record keeping. Save Session to resume later.
