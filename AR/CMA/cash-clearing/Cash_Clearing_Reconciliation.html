How to use this tool (daily workflow)
  1. Select the store (auto-detected from the schedule upload when possible) and enter the cash date.
  2. In CDK, run Accounting Schedule 1 — CASH CLEARING (no zero-balance controls, include post-ahead), export to Excel, and upload the file under CDK Schedule.
  3. From the payment processor site, export the day's transactions and upload the file under CC Report.
  4. Enter the actual deposit totals by type.
  5. Click Run Unfound Match — CC transactions not on the schedule appear under Not On Schedule. Fix any wrong reference numbers there (the amount/note re-match as you type).
  6. For schedule items paid outside the processor, type Y (or the actual amount) in the Received column. CC-matched rows are received automatically.
  7. When the Variance is 0.00, click Generate Posting, then copy the AGJE lines and paste into CDK AGJE (Expert Mode, right-click → Paste).
  8. Click Export Record to download the reconciliation record for record keeping. Save Session lets you resume later.
